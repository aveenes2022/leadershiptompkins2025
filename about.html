About Us

We are Leadership Tompkins,
a group committed to giving back to our community.
As part of our community service project,
we created this website to make it easy for people to find donation drop-off locations and see what items are most needed.

goal is to bring people together through generosity and kindness.
Whether you’re donating food, hygiene products, or clothing,
every contribution makes a difference.
Thank you for being a part of this effort—we appreciate your support!
